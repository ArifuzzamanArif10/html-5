Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines programmed to think and learn like humans. It enables machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making. AI is rapidly transforming various industries, from healthcare and finance to transportation and entertainment. While AI offers numerous benefits like increased efficiency and accuracy, it also raises ethical concerns about job displacement and potential misuse. AI's impact is already visible in everyday life, from virtual assistants like Siri and Alexa to personalized recommendations on streaming services. In healthcare, AI assists in early disease detection and drug discovery. In finance, it helps with fraud detection and algorithmic trading. Moreover, AI is revolutionizing industries like manufacturing and logistics through automation and optimized processes.
